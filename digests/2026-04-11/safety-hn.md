## Community Sentiment

The Hacker News AI safety community appears increasingly polarized and concerned:

- **Skepticism about corporate claims**: Heavy criticism of both OpenAI's liability legislation and Anthropic's security marketing suggests growing distrust of AI company narratives
- **Alarm over escalating tensions**: The violence against Altman prompted serious discussions about the risks of AI development becoming a target for extremist actions
- **Technical scrutiny intensifying**: Community members are demanding more rigorous validation of AI capability claims, particularly around security applications
- **Governance anxiety**: Multiple stories about regulatory battles and liability frameworks indicate deep uncertainty about how AI oversight should work
