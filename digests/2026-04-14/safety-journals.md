# Journal Articles (2026-04-14)

## Key Articles

**[Interpretability and implicit model semantics in biomedicine and deep learning](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-026-01177-0)** - *Nature Machine Intelligence*

This article examines the critical intersection of model interpretability and implicit semantic structures in biomedical AI applications. The research explores how deep learning models develop internal representations that may not align with human understanding of biological processes, creating significant challenges for both model validation and clinical deployment. The findings have important implications for AI safety in high-stakes domains, particularly highlighting the need for robust interpretability methods that can uncover potentially dangerous misalignments between model behavior and intended biological reasoning before deployment in healthcare settings.
