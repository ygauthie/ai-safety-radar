# Journal Articles (2026-04-13)

## Key Articles

**[Interpretability and implicit model semantics in biomedicine and deep learning](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-026-01177-0)** - *Nature Machine Intelligence*

This article explores the critical challenge of understanding implicit semantics within deep learning models, particularly in high-stakes biomedical applications. The research addresses how complex neural networks develop internal representations that may not align with human-interpretable concepts, creating potential safety risks in medical AI systems. The work has significant implications for AI alignment research, as it highlights the gap between model performance and human understanding of model reasoning, which is crucial for deploying AI systems safely in domains where incorrect decisions can have life-or-death consequences.
